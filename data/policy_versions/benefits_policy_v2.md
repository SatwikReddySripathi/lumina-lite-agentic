## Health Insurance

### Medical Coverage

All full-time employees are eligible for medical insurance on the first day of employment. We offer three plan options:

- **Premium Plan**: $0 employee contribution, $250 deductible, 90% coverage
- **Standard Plan**: $50/month contribution, $500 deductible, 80% coverage  
- **Basic Plan**: $100/month contribution, $1000 deductible, 70% coverage

**Dependents:** You may add spouse and children under 26 to your plan. Dependent coverage costs $150/month for spouse, $75/month per child.

### Dental Coverage

Dental insurance is included at no cost for employees. Coverage includes:
- Preventive care: 100% covered (cleanings, exams)
- Basic procedures: 80% covered (fillings, extractions)
- Major procedures: 50% covered (crowns, root canals)

**Dependents:** Add dependents for $30/month per person.

### Vision Coverage

Vision insurance covers one eye exam per year and $150 toward glasses or contacts. Employee cost: $15/month. Add dependents for $10/month each.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

### Vacation Days

PTO accrual is based on years of service:

- **0-2 years:** 15 days per year
- **3-5 years:** 20 days per year
- **6+ years:** 25 days per year

PTO accrues each pay period. Maximum carryover: 40 days.

### Sick Days

Separate from PTO, employees receive 10 sick days per year. Unused sick days do not roll over.

### Holidays

CVS observes 10 paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Memorial Day
- Juneteenth *(newly added)*
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas (2 days)
- One floating holiday

## Life Insurance

CVS provides basic life insurance at no cost:
- **Coverage Amount:** 1x annual salary (up to $250,000)
- **Accidental Death & Dismemberment:** Same amount

**Supplemental Life Insurance:** Purchase additional coverage up to 5x salary at employee expense.

## Disability Insurance

### Short-Term Disability

Covers 60% of salary for up to 90 days if unable to work due to illness or injury. No waiting period.

### Long-Term Disability

Covers 60% of salary after 90 days, up to age 65 or recovery. Provided at no cost to employees.
