## Health Insurance

### Medical Coverage

All full-time employees are eligible for medical insurance on the first day of employment. We offer three plan options:

- **Premium Plan**: $0 employee contribution, $250 deductible, 90% coverage
- **Standard Plan**: $50/month contribution, $500 deductible, 80% coverage  
- **Basic Plan**: $100/month contribution, $1000 deductible, 70% coverage

**Dependents:** You may add spouse and children under 26 to your plan. Dependent coverage costs $150/month for spouse, $75/month per child.

### Dental Coverage

Dental insurance is included at no cost for employees. Coverage includes:
- Preventive care: 100% covered (cleanings, exams)
- Basic procedures: 80% covered (fillings, extractions)
- Major procedures: 50% covered (crowns, root canals)

**Dependents:** Add dependents for $30/month per person.

### Vision Coverage

Vision insurance covers one eye exam per year and $150 toward glasses or contacts. Employee cost: $15/month. Add dependents for $10/month each.

## Retirement Benefits

### 401(k) Plan

CVS Health offers a 401(k) retirement savings plan with company match:

- **Employee Contribution:** Up to 50% of salary (IRS limits apply)
- **Company Match:** 50% match on first 6% of salary contributed
  - Example: If you contribute 6%, CVS contributes 3%
- **Vesting Schedule:** 
  - Company match vests immediately (0-year cliff)
  - You are always 100% vested in your own contributions

**Investment Options:** 20+ mutual funds, target-date funds, and CVS stock.

### Pension Plan

Employees hired before January 1, 2020 are enrolled in the traditional pension plan. New employees are not eligible.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

### Vacation Days

PTO accrual is based on years of service:

- **0-2 years:** 15 days per year
- **3-5 years:** 20 days per year
- **6+ years:** 25 days per year

PTO accrues each pay period. Maximum carryover: 40 days.

### Sick Days

Separate from PTO, employees receive 10 sick days per year. Unused sick days do not roll over.

### Holidays

CVS observes 10 paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas (2 days)
- One floating holiday

## Parental Leave

### Maternity Leave

Birth mothers receive 12 weeks of fully paid leave.

### Paternity Leave

Non-birth parents receive 4 weeks of fully paid leave.

### Adoption Leave

Parents adopting a child receive 8 weeks of fully paid leave.

## Professional Development

### Tuition Reimbursement

CVS reimburses up to $5,000 per year for job-related courses at accredited institutions. Requirements:
- Must achieve grade of B or higher
- Submit receipts within 90 days of course completion
- Remain employed for 12 months after reimbursement or repay amount

### Conference Attendance

Employees may attend up to 2 professional conferences per year. CVS covers registration, travel, and accommodation. Manager approval required.

### Certifications

CVS pays for one professional certification exam per year (e.g., AWS, PMP, Scrum Master). Study materials not included.

## Wellness Benefits

### Gym Membership

CVS reimburses up to $50/month for gym membership. Submit receipts quarterly.

### Mental Health

Employees and dependents have access to 6 free counseling sessions per year through our Employee Assistance Program (EAP).

### Wellness Incentives

Participate in wellness activities to earn up to $500/year:
- Annual physical exam: $100
- Health screening: $100
- Wellness challenges: $300 (varies by activity)

## Life Insurance

CVS provides basic life insurance at no cost:
- **Coverage Amount:** 1x annual salary (up to $250,000)
- **Accidental Death & Dismemberment:** Same amount

**Supplemental Life Insurance:** Purchase additional coverage up to 5x salary at employee expense.

---

## Disability Insurance

### Short-Term Disability

Covers 60% of salary for up to 90 days if unable to work due to illness or injury. No waiting period.

### Long-Term Disability

Covers 60% of salary after 90 days, up to age 65 or recovery. Provided at no cost to employees.
